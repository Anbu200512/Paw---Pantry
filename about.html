Our Story

Born In A Kitchen,
Raised With Love

It all started in 2018 when our founder Sarah couldn't find a single treat on the shelf that she felt good about giving her rescue Golden Retriever, Bailey. Filled with fillers, artificial colors, and ingredients she couldn't pronounce — she decided to take matters into her own hands.

What began as small batches in a home kitchen quickly became a neighborhood favorite. Friends, family, and eventually strangers started asking for "those homemade treats." Today, Paw Pantry is a team of passionate pet lovers dedicated to transforming the way we feed our four-legged family members.

Our Journey

How We
Grew

2018

The Beginning

Founded in a small kitchen with a rescued Golden Retriever named Bailey as our first taste-tester. Our first batch made just 50 treats.

2020

Going National

Expanded nationwide shipping and partnered with 50+ independent pet stores. Our team grew from 2 to 15 passionate pet lovers.

2023

Shelter Program

Launched "One Bag, One Meal" initiative — donating over 100K meals to shelters across the country. Became a certified B Corporation.

2025

1 Million Tails

Celebrated our millionth happy pup and launched our first organic line. Now a team of 50+ dedicated to transforming pet nutrition.
