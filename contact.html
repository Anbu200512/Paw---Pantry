Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

What are your shipping options?
We offer free standard shipping on orders over $35. Express shipping (2-3 business days) is available for a flat $7.99. We ship to all 50 states and military APO/FPO addresses.
Are your treats safe for puppies?
Yes! Our soft training bites and puppy-sized biscuits are specially formulated for dogs aged 8 weeks and older. Always supervise your pup during treat time, and introduce new treats gradually.
Do you have a satisfaction guarantee?
Absolutely! If your dog doesn't love our treats, we'll refund your order — no questions asked. That's the Paw Pantry promise! Just reach out to us within 30 days of purchase.
Where are your ingredients sourced?
We source all ingredients from trusted US farms and global partners who share our commitment to quality. Our meats are always free-range or wild-caught, and our produce is non-GMO and pesticide-free.
What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be at least half-full. Contact our support team and we'll send you a prepaid return label — no hassle, no fuss.
